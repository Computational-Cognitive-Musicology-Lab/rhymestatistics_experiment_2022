Thanks again!

By participating in this research, you’ll be helping us understand how and why humans perceive rhymes—in particular, a form of rhyme known as assonance. Assonant rhymes occur when poets reuse certain vowels. The main difference between the poetry and prose you just heard was how vowels were being reused. In the poetry examples, the poets often use the same sequences of vowels in certain places in the poetic lines.
